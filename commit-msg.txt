fix: replace pwsh.exe with powershell.exe + NoProfile everywhere

Three files were spawning pwsh.exe (PowerShell 7 Core), which
caused a persistent visible popup on every AEGIS startup and
restart attempt since pwsh dies immediately without the handshake.

src/worker/manager.ts: pwsh.exe -> powershell.exe + -NoProfile
src/system/elevation.ts: pwsh.exe -> powershell.exe + -NoProfile
src/context/engine.ts: pwsh.exe -> powershell.exe + -NoProfile

-NoProfile prevents GREGORE profile from outputting text to stdout
before the JSON handshake, which was also causing IPC parse failures.
