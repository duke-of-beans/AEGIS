feat(elev): AEGIS-ELEV-01 — elevation gate and graceful degradation

Add checkIsElevated() utility (src/system/elevation.ts) with session-level
caching. Guard all privileged IPC operations in ProfileManager.applyProfile()
— process priority, service management, power plan, QoS, and memory trim are

without elevation. isElevated added to SystemSnapshot type and populated by
StatsCollector. Status window shows amber indicator when resource control is
inactive.
